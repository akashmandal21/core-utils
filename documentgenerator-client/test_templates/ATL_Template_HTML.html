Confidentiality & Mutual exclusivity clause

Both parties will not disclose the terms of this ATL to any person(s) or companies and/or enter into any commercial discussions for the Leased Premises with any other party for 3 months from the date of signing of this document without the consent of the other party

Rent Commencement

Rents will commence on the later of the Rent Start Date and the date of fulfillment of all the following conditions:

  1. Date of completion of the Rent-Free Fit-out Period post completion of the Lessors’ Scope of Work and date of signing of the Premises Handover Document
  2. Provision of government approvals for the Leased Premises to be occupational and operational
  3. Execution / Registration of Lease Deed.

The above are conditions precedent for the commencement of lease rentals.

Rent Deferment Clause

The Lessor agrees that in case there is a delay in delivering the possession to the Lessee beyond the Leased Premises Handover Date, the Lessor shall give one rent free day for every day of delay.

In case the delay in delivering the possession for fit-outs / operation dates exceed one (1) month from the dates as agreed in this ATL, the Lessee shall have the right to terminate the Agreement to Lease and on such termination the Lessor shall be liable to:

  1. Refund the IFRSD & Advance Rent, or any other deposits/payments made by the Lessee to the Lessor till date along with interest at the rate of 18% per annum till the actual date of payment to the Lessee, and
  2. Amount equivalent to the capex invested by the Lessee in immovable assets in the premises as per the actual cost incurred.

Termination Clause
  • During the Tenure of the Lease, the Lessee can be terminated by the Lessor ONLY on account of non-regular payment of Rents by the Lessee and the inability of the Lessee to cure such non-regular payment over a 30-day period.   • The Lessee can terminate the Lease Deed any time after giving 3 (three) months prior notice in writing to the Lessor  without assigning any reason post completion of the Lessee Lock-in Period

Breach of Obligations by Lessor

If the Lessor(s) causes or has breached any of its obligations, representations and/or warranties stipulated under this document, in any manner whatsoever, ("Breach") the Lessor(s)  shall be liable to pay 5x the amount of Token Deposit ("Default Charges") and/or shall be liable for specific performance of this ATL, in the sole discretion of the Lessee together with the right to get the said error rectified , if possible at Lessor’s costs and/or also to claim additional costs, charges, expenses and losses from the Lessor(s).

Indemnity

The Lessor shall indemnify the Lessee for any and all damage, claims, losses, expenses and Liabilities that may arise due to any act or omission or misrepresentation or breach of any terms or covenants by the Lessor as to title, building approvals and statutory approvals or title of the said property being defective or due to adverse consequence arising out of breach of all or any of the statutory approvals that has been obtained and to be obtained in respect of the premises.

Termination Rights

Both parties shall have the right to terminate this ATL forthwith without any notice, in case of:

  1. Material breach of the any of the obligations or provisions stipulated under this ATL by the other party, including but not limited to violation of the deliverables on the stipulated dates;
  2. Non-delivery of the Leased Premises on the Property Handover Date or the on the expiry of any grace period, as the case may be
  3. Either party is declared insolvent or bankrupt
